about us

ASCC FOR HELP is a charitable student activity, established at Misr International University at 1999, that performs many charitable works in different ways in order to help people and provid them a better life and the students does the charity work in various governorates of Egypt.

There is wide variety of the charity work that the MIU students do in the student activity ASCC such as:

  1. Charitable convoys to provide aid for free.
  2. Distribution of food bags to families who are unable to.
  3. Preparing and distributing meals to the needy.
  4. Sorting and distributing new clothes in good condition and distributing blankets for the winter.
  5. The distribution Ramadan bags.
  6. Donation to non-integrated hospitals.
  7. Establishing a Paralympic Games for children with special needs.

And aside the charity work and the financial aid that we provide, we do our best to spread the joy among children and provide them with their simplest rights such as joy and happines and also:

  1. Conducting awareness campaigns and presentations for children in a fun and simplified manner to raise awareness of diseases and teach them the importance of personal hygiene.
  2. Providing doctors and medicine to children on a permanent basis to check on their health.
  3. Visiting children on a permanent and daily basis in orphanages and playing with them, as well as talking and knowing their dreams and requests to provide them with assistance and psychological support before financial.
  4. Organizing recreational and cultural trips for children to bring joy and pleasure to them.
  5. Visiting the children at Hospital 57375 and Abu Al-Rish and making donations constantly to help the children in their treatment journey, to play with them and to ease the suffering of their sick
