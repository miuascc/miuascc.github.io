Achievements

  • ASCC has succeeded in carrying out more than 100 aid convoys in the governorates of Delta and Upper Egypt.
  • Establishing awareness campaigns about diseases and wrong habits for people and families in rural Egypt.
  • Equipping non-integrated homes with furniture, electrical appliances and ceiling to provide a decent life for people in the Delta and Upper Egypt.
  • Preparing and distributing 6,900 food bags and Ramadan bags to the families and needy people in the Delta and Upper Egypt.
  • Distribution of 6,000 blankets in the winter to 6,000 homes and families in the governorates of Egypt and Cairo.
  • Holding an annual clothing exhibition event to distribute clothes and spread joy among parents and children in the villages and governorates of Egypt.
  • ASCC succeeded, thanks to God, in preparing and organizing weddings for 100 orphan bride and groom, and helped them to raise their families and their new lives.
  • ASCC's work also varied in the areas of reconstruction, such as rehabilitating schools, installing roofs and water connections, and equipping homes with the necessary furniture and equipment.
  • We donated more than 1,800 bags of blood to the Blood Bank of the Ministry of Health and the Red Crescent.
  • Donated equipment and devices to the National Cancer Institute in Sayeda Zainab.
  • Donating and cooperating with the Misr El Kheir Association to relieve the anguish of those in debt and return to normal life, another time for the in debt.
  • Organizing a breast cancer awareness marathon and holding the Autism Challenge Festival in cooperation with the Ministry of Youth and Sports.
  • ASCC succeeded in participating in the YLF Youth Leaders Competition, reaching the qualifying stage among student activities, and choosing it among the top ten student activities in Egypt, based on the decision of the jury and the organizers of the YLF competition, including the Minister of Higher Education, Dr. Khaled Abdel Ghaffar, and the famous businessman Ahmed Abu Hashima, and a member of the Peopleâ€™s Assembly, Mr. Ahmed Fathi.
